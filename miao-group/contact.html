Welcome to the MIAO Group!

The Mathematical Insights into Algorithms for Optimization (MIAO) group is always interested in exceptionally gifted researchers who can design, code up, and analyze unreasonably efficient algorithms for NP-hard optimization problems.

We are using sophisticated mathematics to construct combinatorial solvers that can compete with, and sometimes beat, the best available commercial tools today, and that can in addition output machine-verifiable proofs that their computations are correct. The last few years we have been on a roll, winning several awards for our research. In order to keep up this work and improve it further, we need to find new group members with a passion for both mathematics and programming.

More information about the MIAO research group can be found at www.jakobnordstrom.se/miao-group/#about. At www.jakobnordstrom.se/openings/ you can see if we we have any current openings, or when we are next hiring.

Please don't hesitate a second to shoot an e-mail to jn@di.ku.dk or jakob.nordstrom@cs.lth.se if you have any questions!
